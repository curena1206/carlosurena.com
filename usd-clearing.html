USD Clearing – Commercialization Playbook

1. Purpose & Scope

The objective of this playbook is to provide an end‑to‑end framework to monetize, launch, and scale USD Clearing services for financial‑institution (FI) clients. It covers product design, pricing, onboarding, compliance, and performance governance—serving as a “user manual” for Product, Sales, Operations, and Risk teams.

2. Market & Competitive Landscape

2.1 Demand Drivers

  • Global trade settlement in USD (~88% of FX trades touch USD)
  • Correspondent banking de‑risking—regional banks outsourcing USD nostro
  • Need for intraday liquidity & real‑time balance views (Basel III LCR)

3. Product Architecture & Pricing Design

3.1 Core Components

  • Channels: SWIFT MT/MX, Fedwire, Book‑transfer
  • Ancillary: FX auto‑convert, Intraday credit, MT103 copy
  • Data Services: API for real‑time balances + webhook alerts

3.3 Bundled Discounts

  • Deposit Offset Program: Maintain ≥$10 MM MMDA balance → 50% wire fee rebate
  • Liquidity Bundle: Sweep + Intraday Credit → outbound fee −$0.50
  • Data Bundle: Subscribe to API → free MT199 repair alerts

3.4 Margin Simulation (Tool Spec)

Sales dashboard input fields: volume, corridor, FX %, balance offset. Outputs: Net revenue, ROE, breakeven volume. Prototype built in Excel and embedded as PowerBI widget.

6. KPI & Governance Dashboard

  • Revenue/Client target: $50K/month median by Q4 Yr1
  • STP Rate goal: ≥95%
  • Return/Repair threshold: <1.2%
  • Funding Spread: ≥35 bps over SOFR
Dashboard Spec: PowerBI pulling from ledger + SWIFT logs; refreshed hourly; red‑amber‑green thresholds.

7. Launch & Risk Management

7.1 Phased Rollout

  • Pilot (0–90 days): 3 FI clients, 2 corridors; weekly steering call
  • Phase 2 (90–180 days): Extend to 10 RMs across Americas + EMEA
  • Full Launch (6–12 months): Self‑service onboarding, automated billing

7.2 Risk & Controls

  • OFAC/AML realtime screening (vendor: LexisNexis)
  • Sanctioned corridor exposure limit dashboard
  • Monthly margin review to adjust tier pricing
