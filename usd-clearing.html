Commercialization Plan for USD Clearing

1. Executive Summary

This plan outlines a framework to commercialize USD clearing services tailored for U.S. and global financial institutions. The strategy focuses on pricing transparency, tiered value delivery, and scalable onboarding while supporting compliance and funding goals.

2. Market Landscape

  • Benchmark vs top 5 peer banks (fees, SLAs)
  • SWIFT messaging compatibility (MT/MX)
  • Demand segmentation: Tier 1 FI, Tier 2/3, NBFI

3. Product & Pricing Architecture

The USD Clearing product architecture is structured to deliver transparency, cost-efficiency, and value alignment across client types. Our approach includes three core pricing tiers and modular add-ons tailored to client behavior and usage patterns.

  • Fee Structures:
    • Standard: $8 flat per transaction (SWIFT MT wire format)
    • Volume-based Tiering: $8 → $6 per txn once monthly volume exceeds 5,000 txns
    • Premium Bundle: $10 flat includes real-time repair notifications + monthly SLA report
  • Pricing Drivers:
    • Channel type: Fedwire vs SWIFT vs internal book transfer
    • FX Conversion: Auto-convert feature markup (e.g., 25bps)
    • Operational Impact: High return/repair rate triggers surcharge (e.g., $15 per returned txn)
  • Bundling Strategy:
    • Bundle with MMDA/CD deposit product — offset clearing fees if minimum balances are met
    • Discounted rate if combined with liquidity tools like overnight sweep
    • One-click upgrade path for custody clients to integrate reporting APIs
  • Margin Simulation Tool:
    • Used by Sales to simulate client-level economics during pricing discussions
    • Inputs: avg txn volume, FX share %, return rate, corridor region

Example: A broker-dealer clearing 3,000 monthly wires through SWIFT with a 20% FX conversion rate would fall into Tier 1 ($8/txn), but qualify for the MMDA fee waiver if they maintain $10M in monthly average deposits. Their blended revenue per client could reach $24K/month.

4. Client Segmentation

  • Client type: Correspondent bank, broker-dealer, custody provider
  • Geography: Onshore/offshore USD corridors
  • Behavior: STP %, return rate, volume predictability

5. Enablement Strategy

  • Build playbooks for Relationship Managers and Sales
  • Develop pricing calculator and margin estimator tool
  • Client onboarding kit: FAQs, SLA schedule, pricing sheet

6. KPIs & Reporting

  • Revenue per client (monthly, quarterly)
  • Volume trends vs pricing tier
  • Return/repair rate, STP %, funding spreads

7. Launch Strategy

  • Pilot: 3 existing FI clients across 2 corridors
  • Phase 2: Expand via regional RM enablement
  • Full Launch: All U.S. and EMEA clients with self-service pricing updates
